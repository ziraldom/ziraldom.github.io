What you need to know about me

From the top, you can already tell my name is Matteo. I am currently a Freshman at Michigan State University who is majoring in the Games and Interactive Media major. I've been playing video games ever since I can remember. My first console was the Wii one of my aunts got me for Christmas. Don't ask what year, I don't remember... However, that was the starting point of my journey. Now I find myself at MSU to try and become a game developer. I believe my strengths are art and writing. Coding... I think I'm starting to understand but having trouble writing. As for future plans, a couple of my friends want to try and make an Indie game. Over here at MSU, I believe I'll get some experience to help out and move the project forward. I don't know what the future holds, but we'll get there one step at a time.

Other things about me: I am a pretty big movie watcher and enjoyer. I sometimes read when I find the time. I like to draw. However, this site is primarily focused on games. Other hobbies such as those will only be mentioned here in this paragraph. For further information of myself and games, just scroll down to the next section.

Myself & Games

Since my first console was the Wii, you could say I've been a Nintendo boy. That title can't be denied as I've only ever had Nintendo consoles... But that doesn't mean I've only played Mario or other well-known mascots like Pokemon. No, I've played a variety of games. Only condition is they must be playable on a Nintendo console.

However, I've found that I mainly stick to these genres: RPG, Visual Novel, Action, Platformer. You'll see examples of each one in the next section labeling some of my favorite games. Why do I gravitate to these genres? I like stories, characters, and the fictional world it takes place in. Personal preference probably also plays a role here. However, I think these few genres capture that very well. Oh! We also can't forget about getting attached to the characters if the writing is just that good... surely not.
